Term Insurance Plans

Term insurance is the purest and most affordable form of life insurance. It provides high life cover at low premium and ensures your family is financially protected in your absence.

LIC Jeevan Amar (Plan No. 955)

Key Features

  • Pure term insurance plan with no maturity benefit
  • High sum assured at affordable premiums
  • Option of Level Cover and Increasing Cover
  • Special premium rates for women and non-smokers
  • Tax benefits under Section 80C and 10(10D)

Benefits

  • Death Benefit: Sum Assured payable to nominee on death during the policy term
  • Increasing Cover Option: Sum Assured increases by 10% annually (up to double)
  • Accidental Death & Disability rider available

Eligibility

  • Entry Age: 18–65 years
  • Policy Term: 10–40 years
  • Minimum Sum Assured: ₹25 Lakh
  • Maximum Sum Assured: No limit

Endowment Plans

Endowment plans combine protection with savings. You get life cover during the policy term and a guaranteed maturity amount at the end. Perfect for those who want insurance + savings.

New Jeevan Anand (Plan No. 915)

Key Features

  • Combination of endowment and whole life plan
  • Life cover continues even after maturity (unique feature)
  • Guaranteed additions of ₹50 per ₹1,000 Sum Assured per year
  • Bonus declared annually by LIC
  • Excellent plan for long-term wealth creation

Benefits

  • Death Benefit (during term): Sum Assured + Bonuses
  • Maturity Benefit: Sum Assured + Guaranteed Additions + Bonuses
  • Death Benefit (after maturity): Basic Sum Assured payable again
  • Loan facility available after 3 years

Eligibility

  • Entry Age: 18–50 years
  • Policy Term: 15–35 years
  • Minimum Sum Assured: ₹1,00,000
  • Maximum Sum Assured: No limit

Loan Protection Plans

Protect your family from loan liabilities. If something happens to you, the insurance covers the outstanding loan amount so your family isn't burdened.

LIC New Bima Kiran (Plan No. 950)

Key Features

  • Decreasing term insurance linked to your loan amount
  • Sum Assured decreases as your loan balance reduces
  • Very affordable premiums
  • Covers home loans, personal loans, and other liabilities
  • Single premium or regular premium options

Benefits

  • Death Benefit: Outstanding loan amount covered
  • Family doesn't inherit debt liability
  • Can be used for any loan — home, personal, business

Eligibility

  • Entry Age: 18–60 years
  • Policy Term: Equal to loan tenure
  • Sum Assured: Equal to loan amount
